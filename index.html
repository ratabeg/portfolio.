Frame of Mind

Frame of Mind is a puzzle platformer where the player controls a character who is trapped within the depths of his own psyche in search of a way out. However, in order to do so he must understand who they are and how do they perceive the world. By encountering alternate versions of himself called frames, the character can switch minds with alternate versions to see the world from different perspectives. By changing perspectives, the player can change the reality of the game world to help solve puzzles and progress through the story.
